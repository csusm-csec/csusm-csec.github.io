    C0UG4R5 CTF

    Apply as a challenge creator or join as a player!

    Every semester the CSUSM Cybersecurity Club puts on C0UG4R5 Capture the Flag. C0UG4R5 CTF is a cybersecurity competition designed to test students knowledge in categories like web exploitation, password cracking, reverse engineering, and more. We're constantly looking for challenge developers, local sponsors, and new players! If you have any questions feel free to reach out.
